Engineering College, Nowgong is a newly established college at Nowgong, Distt.-Chhatarpur (MP) by Madhya Pradesh Government under autonomous category. The college is initially located at Nowgong Polytechnic Campus and will be shifted to new building in future for which around 200 acre land is already allocated by Govt. of MP near Naura Pahari (behind Navodaya School) on NH 75 around 5 km from Nowgong. The engineering college, Nowgong initially offers two branches namely civil engineering and mechanical engineering with an intake of 60 in each branch . The number of courses offered will increase in coming years. The college has state-of-art facilities like computer center, library, well equipped labs in each department and other amenities as per AICTE recommendations. The institute has got the approval from AICTE new Delhi since academic session 2012-13. The admission in first year of Bachelor of Engineering will be through entrance test PEPT conducted by Vyavashaik Pariksha Mandal Bhopal and all India exam JEE (Main) conducted by CBSE New Delhi. The Counselling will be conducted by Director of Technical Education, Satpura Bhawan Bhopal. The Institute has been inaugurated on 30th January 2013 by Honourable Shri Shiv Raj Singh Chauhan Ji, Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh. The Institute is having Excellent library, Language Lab, Central Computer Centre along with laboratory facilities.
